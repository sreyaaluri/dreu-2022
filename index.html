research overview
Accurate and detailed cartographic representations of hydrologic streamlines are critical in various scientific domains, such as assessing the quantity and quality of present and future water resources, modeling climate changes, evaluating agricultural suitability, mapping flood inundation, and monitoring environmental changes. My research pertains to training and testing a convolutional neural network that can be used for the detection of fine-scale hydrological streamlines.
